# Medical Disclaimer

**Last Updated:** 27 February 2026

## Training Tool Only

Muninn is a **training platform**. It is not intended for clinical use.

## Not for Clinical Diagnosis

Do not use Muninn or any content within it to make clinical decisions about patient care. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified professionals with access to complete clinical information.

## No Accreditation

Muninn does not provide:

- Formal qualifications or certification
- Equivalence to FRCR or any RCR examination
- CPD credits
- Evidence of clinical competency

Performance in Muninn mock exams does not predict real exam performance.

## Content Accuracy

All case content (DICOM images, model answers, teaching points) is created by your department, not by the Muninn developer. Your department is responsible for the accuracy of any content they create.

## AI Feedback

AI-generated feedback is an educational aid only. It may contain errors and does not represent expert medical opinion.

## Your Responsibility

You are responsible for your own learning and clinical practice. Muninn supplements but does not replace supervised training, formal education, or professional development.
