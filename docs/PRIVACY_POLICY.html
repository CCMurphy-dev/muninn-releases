Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27 February 2026

Summary

Muninn is a locally-installed desktop application. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.

What We Collect

Nothing. Muninn runs entirely on your local systems or department network. No data is transmitted to the developer.

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports
  • No usage tracking
  • No account registration

Your Data

All data in Muninn (trainee records, cases, exam submissions) is:

  • Created and managed by your department
  • Stored on your local systems or department network share
  • Controlled entirely by your department
  • Never accessible to the developer

Third-Party Services

If you choose to enable AI feedback, your configured AI provider will receive the text you submit. This is optional, uses your own API key, and is a direct connection between you and your chosen provider.
