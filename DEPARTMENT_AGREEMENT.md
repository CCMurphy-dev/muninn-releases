## 2. Department Responsibilities

The Licensee Department acknowledges and agrees that it is solely responsible for:

### Data Protection
- Anonymizing all DICOM files before import into the Software
- Ensuring no patient-identifiable information is present in case content
- Managing trainee personal data in accordance with institutional policies and UK GDPR
- Controlling access to the department network share containing training data

### Content Accuracy
- The accuracy and appropriateness of all case content created using the Software
- Model answers, teaching points, and educational materials added to cases
- Ensuring case content is reviewed by appropriately qualified personnel

### Appropriate Use
- Using the Software for educational and training purposes only
- Ensuring users understand the Software is not for clinical diagnosis
- Not representing Software-based assessments as formal qualifications

- All data created by the department (cases, trainee records, exam results) remains the property of the department

## 5. Termination

This agreement terminates automatically if:
- The license period expires without renewal

Upon termination, the department may retain any case content they created.
